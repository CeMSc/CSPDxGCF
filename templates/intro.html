Getting Started

Input 1: Project Information

Provide basic information, such as project location and GCF Results Areas.

Input 2: Project Design Survey

Fill out a short survey to let us understand the level of conflict sensitivity in your project design.

Output A: Project Profile

Using the information you provided, we compare your project's exposure to security risks with its levels of conflict sensitivity and establish if you need to take further actions.

Output B: Tailored Action Points

Based on your readiness levels and risk exposure, we provide preliminary sectorial guidance on how to mitigate risks and promote integrated climate security programming.
